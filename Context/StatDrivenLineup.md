The Stat-Driven Lineup Structure
Order 	Role	Primary Stats to Use	Rationale
1	The Catalyst	Highest OBP, High Speed	Starts the game with the highest probability of getting on base.
2	The Best All-Around	Highest OPS/wOBA	Most plate appearances among top hitters; drives in runs and gets on base.
3	The High-Value Bat	High OPS, Good Avg	Hits with runners on, high probability of extra-base hits.
4	The Cleanup	Highest SLG/HR, RBI	Maximizes RBI potential, often hits with runners on base.
5	The Second Cleanup	High OPS, Balanced	Protects the 4-hole hitter, high RBI potential.
6	The Second Lead-off	High OBP, Good Speed	Starts rallies for the bottom half of the order.
7	The Mid-Range	Moderate OPS/Avg	Balanced hitter to bridge the gap in the order.
8	The Lower Tier	Low Average, High Defense	Often defensive specialist/catcher.
9	The Table Setter	Solid OBP, Speed	Acts as a second lead-off hitter for the top of the lineup.
